Welcome!

I'm Ronan Hix, a PhD student at Princeton University, working with Prof. Eve Ostriker and Prof. Matt Kunz. My work leverages high-performance Numerical Simulations and advanced computational methods to gain insight into the complex physical mysteries of the Interstellar Medium (ISM), with a particular focus on Star Formation Theory. In the emergent post-JWST era, developing a more accurate and comprehensive understanding of star formation is more important than ever, and it remains paramount in our quest to answer some of the biggest open questions in contemporary Astronomy.

The rest of this site is still currently under construction, but reach out if you're interested in learning more about what I do
